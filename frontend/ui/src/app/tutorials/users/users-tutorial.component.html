Invite your teammates

Distr is better with your team. In this tutorial you will invite your colleagues so you can manage your software distribution together.

You can find additional resources on this topic in our docs.

  1. Understand the roles

    Every teammate is assigned a role that defines what they can do in Distr:

    • Viewer – read-only access. Can see applications, deployments and artifacts, but cannot make any changes.
    • User – read and write access. Can create and manage applications, deployments and artifacts.
    • Administrator – full access. Can do everything a User can, plus manage organization settings and invite or remove teammates.

    Learn more about roles and permissions in our RBAC documentation.

  2. Add your teammates

    Enter the name (optional), email and role of each colleague you want to invite, then send the invite. Each teammate will receive an email with a link to join your organization.
