Distr offers subscription plans for different stages of software distribution. This guide explains which plan fits your current needs.

<Aside type="tip">
  All new organizations start with a **30-day Pro Trial**, giving you full
  access to Pro features to evaluate Distr. Learn more in our [trial
  guide](/docs/free-trial/).
</Aside>

## Pro Plan

**Choose Pro to distribute and operate customer installs in production.**

Pro covers everything you need to ship to self-managed customers: deployments through Docker, Kubernetes/Helm, or artifact-based distribution, with version visibility, license enforcement, deployment alerts, and role-based access control across both your team and your customers' teams.

**Ideal for:**

- Shipping your first customer installations and scaling from there
- Teams that need license management and version control
- Organizations requiring role-based access control
- Teams that want login with Google, GitHub, or Microsoft
- Teams that want a branded customer portal

**Key characteristics:**

- Up to 100 customer organizations
- License management for version control
- Role-based access control (Admin, Default, Read-Only)
- Login with Google, GitHub & Microsoft
- Deployment alerts
- Support bundles for remote troubleshooting
- Deployment history and rollbacks
- Custom branding for your Customer Portal
- 7-day log retention

<Aside type="note">
  For detailed feature comparisons and limits, visit our [pricing
  page](/pricing/).
</Aside>

## Business Plan

**Choose Business when you distribute at scale.**

Business removes the customer limit and adds the features you need to run your entire distribution operation on Distr: partner management, license templates, and a fully white-labeled customer experience on your own domain.

**Ideal for:**

- Vendors with a large or fast-growing customer base
- Companies selling through resellers or partners
- Teams standardizing license issuance with templates
- Organizations that want the customer-facing experience fully on their own domain
- Teams where vendors or customers require their own OIDC provider

**Key characteristics:**

- Unlimited customer organizations
- Reseller / partner organizations
- License templates
- Custom domains (full white label)
- Enterprise SSO — bring-your-own OIDC (vendors and customers)
- 5 TB registry storage and 30-day log retention
- Priority support

## Enterprise Plan

**Choose Enterprise when Distr becomes your entire commercial self-hosted delivery suite.**

At this stage you distribute more than software. Documentation, support portals, public images, automated workflows, and usage-based billing all live inside one platform — with the isolation and contractual guarantees your procurement team asks for.

**Ideal for:**

- Organizations using Distr as their complete commercial platform
- Companies needing unlimited scale
- Teams requiring dedicated infrastructure
- Organizations needing advanced features like customer billing and custom roles
- Companies requiring SLA guarantees, custom contracts, and dedicated support

**Key characteristics:**

- Unlimited customer organizations, deployments, and users
- Advanced features: customer billing, custom roles, SAML SSO, workflows
- Documentation pages, wiki, and ticket system
- Public registry and CVE scanning
- Custom log retention
- Dedicated infrastructure
- Custom contracts and procurement support
- Dedicated support engineer and SLA

<Aside type="note">
  Enterprise plans include custom pricing based on your specific needs. [Contact
  us](/contact/) to discuss Enterprise options.
</Aside>

## Making Your Decision

### Start with Pro:

- ✅ Comes with all the essentials you need to ship to self-hosted customers
- ✅ Logs, metrics, and alerts from every deployment
- ✅ License management + RBAC
- ✅ Branded Customer Portal

### Upgrade to Business when:

- ✅ You're integrating Distr deeper into your operation and scaling it up
- ✅ You want the entire experience fully white-labeled: custom domains, white-label emails, and your own OIDC provider
- ✅ You onboard resellers and distribution partners
- ✅ You want license templates for standardized license issuance
- ✅ You need 30-day log retention and priority support

### Consider Enterprise if:

- ✅ You're a security-conscious vendor serving security-conscious and regulated customers
- ✅ You want unlimited usage at a flat yearly rate
- ✅ You require dedicated infrastructure, SAML SSO, and custom roles
- ✅ You need custom contracts, SLAs, and a dedicated support engineer

## Self-Hosting Distr

If you prefer to self-host Distr in your own environment, we offer two editions:

- **Community Edition** - Free and open source, ideal for getting started
- **Enterprise Edition** - Unlocks the features of our paid plans in your own environment

For more information about self-hosting options, deployment methods, and how to get started, see our [self-hosting guide](/docs/self-hosting/).
